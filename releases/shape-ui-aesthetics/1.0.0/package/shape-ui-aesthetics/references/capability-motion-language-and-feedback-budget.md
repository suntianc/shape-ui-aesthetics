# Motion Language & Feedback Budget

**Stable ID:** `motion-language-and-feedback-budget`  
**Class:** Core  
**Primary domain:** Motion, Rhythm & Causality

## Observable trigger

Route here when local animations feel like different products, feedback competes with identity motion, or easing, duration, energy, and state treatment proliferate without semantic roles.

## Non-trigger and near miss

Do not route here for one loader, one broken spatial transition, or a direction with no justified motion. First decide whether motion should exist before systematizing it.

## Decision question

What small semantic motion grammar makes routine behavior quiet, consequential feedback clear, identity recognizable, and emphasis scarce across components and platforms?

## Interpretation method

Audit existing motion or translate accepted product traits into motion traits. Define a quiet base grammar of roles, duration bands, easing families, entry/exit relations, interruptibility, and state behavior. Reserve stronger displacement, elasticity, color, or rhythm for rare high-value states. Treat idle, pending, partial, success, error, cancel, and retry as a truthful feedback contract.

## Executable design procedure

1. Inventory motion by purpose, frequency, state, energy, duration, easing, and platform.
2. Remove unjustified motion and consolidate materially identical behavior.
3. Define named roles for direct feedback, continuity, progress, attention, success, warning, and identity.
4. Establish restrained base ranges and a small easing vocabulary.
5. Allocate a scarce accent budget and specify when it cannot be used.
6. Complete feedback state contracts and reduced/no-motion mappings.
7. Calibrate on repeated use, failures, interruption, target devices, and cross-platform semantics.

## Boundaries and failure modes

- Do not introduce animation merely to have a language.
- Do not make every easing or icon animation an accent.
- Do not omit error, cancellation, retry, or partial states.
- Avoid long repeated microinteractions users must wait through.
- Preserve semantic consistency across platforms without forcing identical numeric values.

## Attached Reference Knowledge

Brand motion can emerge from audit or trait translation, but purpose remains primary. A base of quiet, reusable behavior gives exceptional motion meaning. State feedback is a contract, not a decorative loader.

## Runtime Self-Critique

Ask whether a repeated animation still feels respectful, whether accents remain rare, whether all state outcomes are truthful, and whether the grammar supports rather than replaces the product thesis.

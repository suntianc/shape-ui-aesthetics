Root diagnosis — the absence is substitution-proof

The page has polish but no product-specific authorship. Its most prominent elements establish the category "modern AI workspace," yet none makes a distinct NOVA protagonist or user ritual perceptible. The same gradient headline, dark glass surface, floating status cards, logo strip, generic metrics, and email-conversion dialog could be transferred unchanged to another AI productivity product without changing their meaning.

This is not primarily a spacing, proportion, or color problem. The page lacks a governing relationship between a specific thing NOVA makes present, a consequential action the user performs, and evidence of the change NOVA causes. Polish is carrying the promise that product behavior should carry.

The structural absence is substitution-proof: remove the NOVA wordmark and nothing else identifies a unique product. The headline supplies no protagonist; the preview supplies no observable input-to-output causality; the trust signals supply no product-specific proof; and the acquisition dialog makes signup — not a distinct product act — the primary ritual. These relationships remain absent at desktop, mobile, and interaction states.

Liking or disliking the dark theme, gradient type, glass cards, large headline, rounded controls, or floating motion is preference. A palette swap, tighter spacing, different typeface, fewer cards, or more dramatic animation could alter taste without making the page more authored.

What this diagnosis is not

  • Not a spacing checklist — proportions are competent; authorship is absent
  • Not a palette swap — color hierarchy works; identity is absent
  • Not invented psychology — no user research available; comprehension remains untested
  • Not Aesthetic Acceptance — brand fit and emotional character are human choices
  • Not a redesign brief — one structural relationship must become load-bearing first

Smallest powerful direction

One high-leverage relationship that makes the page authored rather than decorated.

Make one traceable transformation from source-bound brief to decision-backed deliverable the protagonist, using only product truths the existing page already hints at: a project brief, source synthesis, planning, asset creation, launch, and automation.

Governing Proposition

NOVA keeps the evidence and decisions of a project visibly continuous as a team turns a brief into shipped work. The desired afterimage is not "AI made it faster" but "I can see how this outcome came from our context, and I know what to steer next."

Signature Move

A single continuous project object whose evidence, decisions, and deliverable visibly accumulate across Plan → Create → Launch. The visitor should recognize what changed and why — not merely watch a percentage rise.

Why This Is Sufficient

It does not require more feature cards, a new palette, a 3D effect, or a larger demo. It asks one existing relationship to become load-bearing across composition and interaction. If NOVA cannot truthfully preserve sources, decisions, and outputs this way, the product owner must provide a real differentiating mechanism before an authored thesis can be finalized.

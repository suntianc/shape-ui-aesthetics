# Meaning, Culture & Aesthetic Thesis Index

**Decision question:** Has product meaning become a specific experiential proposition that can govern form and behavior?

**Material signals:** The brief relies on visual adjectives; the protagonist or ritual is interchangeable; cultural or emotional meaning must shape the experience; the desired afterimage is unclear.

**Non-signals:** A stable thesis already governs the artifact and the open issue is only layout, implementation, or local polish.

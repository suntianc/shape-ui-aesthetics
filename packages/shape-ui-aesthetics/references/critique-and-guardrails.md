# Critique and Guardrails

## Protect authorship

Ask whether the artifact could belong unchanged to an unrelated product. Trace every prominent visual or behavioral move to the protagonist, ritual, desired afterimage, central tension, or core task. Remove decorative effects that compete with those relationships. Do not mistake cleanliness, trend conformity, or component consistency for authorship.

## Inspect the artifact as a system

Check the whole-page silhouette before local polish. Inspect mass and void, focal order, reading path, value structure, interaction states, temporal hierarchy, and cross-surface grammar. Test the Signature Move in the actual medium; a still description does not prove an interaction or computational effect.

## Carry complexity responsibly

Treat information load and mental burden as a separate quality dimension. Preserve necessary complexity while reducing simultaneous recall, false urgency, needless choice, and competing emphasis. Do not hide essential comparison or operational context merely to make the interface look sparse.

## Trigger verification proportionally

- Verify factual claims that affect state, safety, price, availability, data meaning, or user action.
- Seek accountable human judgment for identity, cultural position, sacred or community-specific mechanisms, and emotional fit.
- Validate text alternatives, keyboard and focus behavior, contrast, target acquisition, zoom/reflow, and reduced-motion behavior when applicable.
- Test real content, cardinality, long labels, empty/loading/error/success states, localization, and consequential devices.
- Measure performance when 3D, shaders, particles, video, large assets, or concurrent motion carry the direction.

Do not claim professional compliance from aesthetic reasoning. Escalate medical, safety, legal, structural, ergonomic, cultural, or accessibility questions to appropriate evidence and expertise.

## Degrade without betraying the idea

Preserve the primary perceptual and task relationship when a device, input mode, performance budget, or accessibility need prevents the ideal implementation. Reduce density, dimensionality, motion, or rendering cost according to the actual constraint, choosing the change that least damages meaning and agency. Supply a truthful static or lower-cost fallback rather than a broken imitation.

## Run Runtime Self-Critique

Before delivery, identify:

1. the strongest evidence of product-specific authorship;
2. the weakest unresolved aesthetic relationship;
3. any medium choice that behaves as decoration rather than structure;
4. any hierarchy or information-load conflict;
5. any interaction, state, accessibility, cultural, or factual obligation still unverified;
6. the smallest consequential correction.

Use this critique to improve the artifact. Do not use it to certify the skill package or grant Aesthetic Acceptance; that judgment belongs to the user.

# Coherence, Critique & Evidence Index

**Decision question:** Do local choices remain one authored visual grammar across components, states, surfaces, and implementations?

**Material signals:** Similar roles look or behave differently; local polish fragments the visual world; tokens encode raw values without semantic relationships; states or platforms drift from the thesis.

**Non-signals:** The grammar is coherent and the unresolved issue belongs to a single factual, interaction, performance, or accessibility defect.

**Selection seams:** Route perceptual and optical review only for a localized perceived-versus-measured discrepancy after the governing composition is accepted. Route motion quality only for an already named temporal job and concrete hypothesis or artifact; signal Motion when purpose is unresolved.

**Cross-domain handoffs:** Signal every domain whose governing relationship is missing or contradicted. Treat grammar as synthesis, not as authority to flatten meaningful domain differences or replace artifact-specific critique.
